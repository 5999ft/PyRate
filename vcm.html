VCM and Maxvar Module¶

This Python module implements covariance calculation and Variance/Covariance matrix functionality. The algorithms are based on Matlab codes ‘cvdcalc.m’ and ‘vcmt.m’ from the Pirate package.

Calculate average covariance versus distance (autocorrelation) and its best fitting exponential function

Parameters:

  • calc_alpha – bool whether you calculate alpha.
